Vishal's Dance Floor is amongst the revered academies for dance in Delhi. A team of well-known and proficient instructors trains a plethora of students in a variety of dance forms. Our academy is also instrumental in organising a host of events followed by fests that feature stage shows. Undoubtedly it is one of the best dance classes in Kalkaji, Delhi .

We conduct classes for various dance forms including Bollywood, B-Boying, Hip-Hop, Locking Popping, Zumba, Kathak, Salsa, Contemporary and Hula Hoop. Catering to almost all age groups, there are special batches for KIDS at this dance studio. One can also avail professional choreography for events like weddings, corporate events, school functions and college festivals. Operational on all days, visit this centre from 10AM till 9PM

Ballet

Ballet originated in the 15th century, first in Italy and then in France. Over the centuries, ballet has influenced many other styles of dance and become a fine art form in its own right.

Hip Hop

Another descendant of jazz dance, hip-hop emerged from the streets of New York in the 1970s at the same time as rap and DJing.It is perhaps the earliest form of hip-hop dance.

Belly Dance

Belly dancing emerged from the folk traditions of the Middle East. Unlike most forms of Western dancing, belly dancing is a solo performance that focuses on the torso and hips.

Flamenco

Flamenco dance is an expressive dance form that mixes percussive footwork with intricate hand, arm, and body movements. It emerged from the cultures of the Iberian Peninsula in the 1700s.

Latin Dance

Latin dance is a broad term for any number of ballroom dance forms that evolved in the 19th centuries in the Spanish-speaking Western Hemisphere. These styles have roots in European and indigenous ritual.

Jazz

Jazz is a lively dance style that relies heavily on originality and improvisation. This style often uses bold, dramatic body movements, including body isolations and contractions.
